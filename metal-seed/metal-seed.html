Company Profile

The Metal (Pvt.) Ltd. is the pioneer and leading company of mechanized cultivation in Bangladesh. The Metal (Pvt.) Ltd was established in 1987. We are the sister concern of Project Builders Limited (PBL). PBL is the well reputed & renowned construction company in Bangladesh.

The Metal (Pvt.) Ltd. is Exclusive Dealer of M/S. Tractors and Farm Equipment Limited (TAFE), Chennai, India. We have been involved in marketing of TAFE Brand Tractors and Implements since 1993 and so far we have sold more than 30,000 (Thirty Thousand) units of Tractors all over Bangladesh. TAFE Tractors are manufactured by our principle in joint collaboration with Massey-Ferguson (MF) of U.K since 1961, they sold Tractor around 90 countries all over the world. We are also the Exclusive Distributor of MASCHIO S.P.A of Italy for Bangladesh. MASCHIO is a World famous Rotary Tiller Manufacturer. We also Exclusive Dealer of CLAAS India Private Limited. CLAAS is a German Based leading manufacturer for various type of Combine Harvester, having 14 production locations worldwide and offering products in more than 140 countries. We also Exclusive Dealer of Asia Technology Co. Ltd. of Korea is an Excellent Rice Transplanter Manufacturer. We are also Dealer of Vietnam Agrotech Co. Ltd. of Vietnam for Rice and Wheat Reaper. We are Exclusive Dealer of BCS India Pvt. Ltd., for their products like Reaper Binder etc, We are Exclusive Dealer of Bull Machine (Pvt.) Ltd. India, they manufactured Backhoe Loader etc.

We are very much concerned with our all products with available genuine spare parts backup and after sale service throughout Bangladesh. We have 21 nos well equipped own 3S (Sales, Service and Spare Parts) combination Office all over Bangladesh with trained personals to carry out repairs of Machinery. More than 50 nos Sub Dealer and 110 nos Spare Parts dealer all over Bangladesh.
